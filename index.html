Glow Run Health Event

"Get ready to light up the night and ignite your passion for health and wellness at our Glow Run Health Event!



Date : 1 June 2024
Time : 4:00 PM until 11:00
Venue: Dataran Jam Besar, Sungai Petani Kedah Darul Aman




EVENT DESCRIPTION

Embrace the classic glow run theme with vibrant neon colors that will light up the night. To create an amazing visual spectacle, participants may wear in neon clothes, accessories, and glow paint.

Join us for an electrifying evening of fitness and fun at the Glow Run Health Event! This unique event combines the excitement of a night run with the importance of health and wellness, all set against the backdrop of vibrant glowing lights.


OBJECTIVE OF THE EVENT

1. Organize event to promote health and wellness.
2. Raise awareness of the importance of mental health to generate global health.
3. Encourage raise fund for charity raise money by asking people to donate and entry fee.
