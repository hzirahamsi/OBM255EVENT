Public Transport Suggestion

Option 1: Train + Taxi/Grab

  • Get the train from where you came from and get off at the nearest train station which is Sungai Petani train station. Then, proceed to Dataran Jam Besar by ordering a taxi or using GrabCar.
  • Takes 5 minutes to arrive from Sungai Petani Train Station.

Option 2: Bus Express

  • Get the bus from your nearest bus terminal which has a trip scheduled to Sungai Petani.
  • Stop at Terminal Jalan Petri and you can arrive at Dataran Jam Besar within 6 minutes by walking.

Option 3: Own Vehicle

  • This option is the best if you are just living around and wanted to save costs.
  • You can directly drive to the event location with the help of the nagivation app such as Waze or Google Maps.
