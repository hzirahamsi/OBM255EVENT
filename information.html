Refund Policy

Due to the substantial costs associated with organizing Glow Fest, we regret to inform you that we are unable to offer refunds for tickets. Refunds will not be provided in the event of rescheduled dates. Additionally, we do not offer refunds for changes of mind or inability to attend the event. However, if you are unable to attend, you may transfer your ticket to another individual or retain it for use at next year’s festival.

Event Rescheduling Policy

Glow Fest reserves the right to reschedule activities at its sole risk.

Events may be rescheduled due to potential impact of weather and safety issues, such as wind, rain, hot or cold temperatures, or drought. If situations allow, the event will be rescheduled on the next available date at the venue. Tickets will be immediately transferred to the rescheduled event date.

Please keep in mind that rescheduling events causes no change in refunds.

If you are unable to attend the rescheduled date, you may transfer your ticket to another person or keep it to use at next year's event.

We regret that we cannot provide refunds for change of mind or difficulty to attend the event.
